Leica M3 · Shutter Variants

The M3 was produced 1954–1966 with two shutter speed sequences.

VariantKey SpeedsSerial #
Early1/25 · 1/50 · 1/100Below 854001
Late ★1/30 · 1/60 · 1/125854001 and above

How to check: Look at your shutter dial. If you see 1/25 and 1/50, select Early. If you see 1/30 and 1/60, select Late.


Changeover at serial #854001 in 1957. Always trust your dial over the production year.
